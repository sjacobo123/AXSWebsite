Welcome!

We are a professional, co-ed fraternity that focuses on the pursuit of higher education while also fostering a strong sense of community. To reinforce these values throughout the year, we teach Splash classes, hand out cookies to students after their exams, host boardgame nights, organize graduate school/scholarship/company panels, send weekly newsletters, and set up several social gatherings each quarter. To keep up with our events and see the most recent newsletter (it will have lab opportunities), please look at the section below. To learn more specifically about what our organization does and stands for, please look at both the about and history pages. To see our past and current members, look at our brothers page and each individual class page. Also, to learn about the recruitment cycle, please check out our join page and see what events we have. Furthermore, if you are curious to learn about our current leadership or want to contact us, please check out our contact page. Overall, thank you for checking us out and have a wonderful day!
